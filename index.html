What is CDN?

A Content Delivery Network (CDN) is a system of distributed servers that deliver content to users based on their geographic location. Using a CDN ensures faster delivery of content, improving both app performance and speed.

In this site, we are using a CDN to load Google Fonts and Bootstrap, ensuring faster load times by retrieving resources from the server closest to you.
